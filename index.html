Solutions

The Platform for Intelligent Deal Flow

Syndesis provides a single, trusted environment to manage every stage of your investment or fundraising journey.

Advanced Discovery & Filtering

Don't search for a needle in a haystack. Our rich, multi-select filters allow you to pinpoint the perfect match based on sector, stage, traction, check size, and dozens of other critical data points.

Direct & Seamless Communication

Move conversations forward, faster. With built-in messaging and booking requests, you can connect, share materials, and schedule meetings without ever leaving the platform, eliminating chaotic email threads.

AI-Powered Notes & Analysis

Go beyond simple note-taking. Our AI assistant helps you structure meeting notes, identify risks, and draft next steps, creating an intelligent, private knowledge base for every opportunity you track.

Built for Professional Diligence

Trust is our foundation. Secure data rooms, verified traction badges, and transparent investor portfolios provide the clarity needed to conduct thorough due diligence with confidence.

Bank-Grade Security & Privacy

Your data is protected by enterprise-level security protocols. Syndesis is built to be GDPR/CCPA-ready, giving you full control over your information with password hashing, optional 2FA, and strict privacy controls.

Investor Clubs & Collaboration

Fundraising and investing are often team sports. Our platform provides the infrastructure for investor clubs and syndicates to share notes privately, collaborate on diligence, and manage deal flow as a cohesive unit.

Your Questions, Answered
  • Who is Syndesis for?

    Syndesis is a two-sided platform designed for early-stage founders seeking capital, and for accredited investors, VCs, and investor clubs seeking high-quality, curated deal flow. It is built for serious players who want to replace noise with signal.

  • How is Syndesis different from other fundraising platforms?

    We focus on three key areas:
    1. Intelligent Matching: Our AI-powered engine goes beyond simple tags to calculate a compatibility score, ensuring highly relevant connections.
    2. Integrated Workflow Tools: With AI-assisted notes, watchlists, and built-in messaging, we provide a mini-CRM for your deal flow, not just a passive listing site.
    3. Market Intelligence: Our live dashboard gives you a real-time view of funding trends and hot sectors, providing a data edge you won't find elsewhere.

  • Is my fundraising data confidential?

    Absolutely. Your privacy and data security are paramount. We use bank-grade security protocols, and you have granular control over your profile. Sensitive information, like data room links or detailed traction metrics, can be gated for visibility only by verified Premium investors.

  • What are Investor Clubs?

    Investor Clubs are private, invitation-only communities on Syndesis. They allow syndicates, angel groups, and VCs to share deal flow, collaborate on due diligence with shared notes, and manage their group's pipeline in a secure, dedicated environment.

  • What is your subscription and cancellation policy?

    Syndesis operates on a flexible subscription basis. You can choose a monthly or annual plan and can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time through your account settings. Upgrades are applied instantly, while downgrades and cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.
